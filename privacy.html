Payment data

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not receive, process or store your full card number, CVV or banking credentials. We store only payment references such as the Stripe PaymentIntent ID, amount, currency and payment status, because these are needed for order fulfilment, support, refunds and accounting.

3. How we use your data

  • Order fulfilment — to generate and deliver your caricature.
  • AI image processing — to analyse the uploaded photo, create a prompt, generate the caricature and save the result temporarily.
  • Photo quality checks — to detect whether a usable human face is present and whether the image is suitable for generation.
  • Content safety checks — to detect and block prohibited requests, including sexual content, exploitation of minors, hate, harassment, graphic violence, illegal activity and deceptive public-figure content.
  • Customer support — to answer questions, manage revisions, issue refunds or resolve failed orders.
  • Fraud and abuse prevention — to protect customers, payment systems, AI providers and our platform.
  • Service improvement — to understand technical errors, improve templates, and monitor quality.
  • Legal and accounting compliance — to meet tax, consumer protection, fraud prevention and record-keeping obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your uploaded photo to train our own AI models. We do not intentionally publish your uploaded photo or generated caricature without your permission.

4. AI processing and content safety checks

Caricature.online uses AI services to help assess uploaded photos, create generation prompts and produce caricature images. This means your uploaded photo and order instructions may be processed automatically by our systems and by selected AI processors strictly for the purpose of fulfilling your order and maintaining platform safety.

What may be checked automatically

  • Whether the uploaded file is a valid image and within accepted limits.
  • Whether the photo contains a visible human face suitable for caricature generation.
  • Whether there are multiple faces when the order requires one clear person photo.
  • Whether the request text contains prohibited or unsafe content.
  • Whether a request requires manual review or cancellation.

Moderation and blocked requests

We may refuse, cancel or refund orders that violate our Terms and Conditions or content safety rules. Where possible, moderation decisions are based on the minimum information needed to detect abuse. We may store a short moderation record, such as the order ID, moderation category, timestamp and blocked reason, for security and dispute-resolution purposes.

Important: Safety checks are designed to protect people and the platform. They are not used to profile you, make unrelated decisions about you, or sell advertising data.

6. Your photo — special notice

Your photo is the most sensitive data we handle. We use it only to process your order, perform safety/quality checks and generate your caricature.

  1. You upload your photo securely over HTTPS.
  2. The photo is stored temporarily in Google Cloud Storage.
  3. The photo may be analysed by AI to check face visibility and generate a better caricature prompt.
  4. The photo may be sent to selected AI processors such as fal.ai and/or Anthropic strictly for order generation and related safety/quality checks.
  5. The generated caricature is saved temporarily and delivered to your email.
  6. Your original photo and generated caricature are scheduled for deletion after 72 hours from delivery.

We do not claim ownership of your original photo. You are responsible for ensuring that you have permission to upload any photo and that each person depicted has consented where required.

9. Your rights

Under GDPR, you may have the following rights. To exercise them, email privacy@caricature.online. We will respond within 30 days unless a lawful extension applies.

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data where legally possible.
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data.
  • Portability — receive certain data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests, including some fraud/safety processing where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for optional cookies or analytics at any time via the Cookie Preference Centre.
  • Lodge a complaint — contact your national data protection authority.

If you are in Greece, you may contact the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (HDPA): www.dpa.gr.

10. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for data transmitted between your browser and our systems.
  • Cloud storage with server-side protection for uploaded and generated files.
  • Restricted access to production systems.
  • Secrets and API keys stored outside public code and managed through secure infrastructure.
  • Stripe-hosted payment processing so we do not handle raw card data.
  • Admin endpoints protected by authentication/secret controls.
  • Logging and monitoring for technical failures and suspicious activity.

No online system is perfectly secure. If a personal data breach occurs and GDPR notification rules apply, we will notify the relevant authority and affected users where required.

11. Children

You must be at least 18 years old to place an order. If an uploaded photo includes a child, you confirm that you are the child’s parent or legal guardian, or that you have explicit permission from the parent or legal guardian.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children for account creation or marketing. Requests that sexualise, exploit, endanger or otherwise misuse images of children are strictly prohibited and may be blocked, cancelled or reported where legally required.
