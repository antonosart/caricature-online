Got questions?

Everything you need to know

Will it actually look like the person?
+
Yes — our AI uses face-swap technology that places the person's actual face into our templates. The likeness comes from the uploaded photo. The better the photo, the better the result. We recommend a clear, front-facing, well-lit portrait.
What if I'm not happy with it?
+
Premium plan includes 1 free revision — just email us within 48 hours. Standard plan: if there's a technical problem on our end, we'll redo it for free. We want every customer to be genuinely happy.
Can I order for multiple people?
+
Absolutely. Select the number of people at the start of the order process and you'll only see templates that fit. Pricing adjusts accordingly — couples from +€5, groups of 3-4 from +€10, 5+ from +€20 on top of the base price.
How quickly will I receive it?
+
Usually within 30–90 seconds of payment. Our AI pipeline processes the caricature immediately. You'll receive a high-resolution PNG directly to your email — no account needed, no waiting around.
Is my photo safe?
+
Completely. Your photo is uploaded with end-to-end encryption to Google Cloud. It's used only to generate your caricature and is automatically and permanently deleted 72 hours after delivery. We never share, sell, or store your images beyond that.
Can I print and frame it?
+
Absolutely — that's what most customers do. All files are delivered at HD (and 4K for Gift Pack) resolution, 300dpi, print-ready. Perfect for A4, A3, or even canvas. Gift Pack includes commercial licence if you want to sell prints.
